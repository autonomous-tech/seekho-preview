What You'll Learn

Four pillars of a confident driver.

Every Seekho student finishes with skills in all four areas. Not just "drive from A to B" — a complete driver.

Road rules & road safety

Pakistani traffic law, DLS Sindh regulations, lane discipline, signal rules, right-of-way. Plus Karachi-specific road culture — rickshaw behaviour, bike swarms, signal logic.

Vehicle anatomy

Tyres, engine basics, fluids, dashboard signs, under-the-hood components. You leave knowing what every warning light means and how to check oil, coolant, and tyre pressure.

Behind-the-wheel (BTW)

Structured progression: parking lot basics → phase streets → main roads → parallel parking → highways → night driving. You move up only when the instructor sees you're ready.

Emergency response

Flat tyre change demo. Jump-start walkthrough. Accident protocol. First-aid basics. What to do if your engine overheats on Shahrah-e-Faisal at 2pm.

Curriculum Breakdown

12 lessons. Two tracks. One complete driver.

Our Teach Me Everything programme splits into a theoretical track (3 sessions + Saturday workshop) and a behind-the-wheel track (9 sessions). Enhance My Skills is a shorter 9-lesson variant for returning drivers.

T Theoretical modules (6 modules + workshop)

01
Introduction to Driver's Education

60-minute session (online or on-site). Programme schedule, road safety primer, handling road anxiety.

02
Vehicle anatomy walkthrough

Under the hood. Engine, coolant, oil, battery. Tyre anatomy and rotation. What every dashboard sign means.

03
Road signs & DLS theory prep

Sindh Police test prep. Road signs flashcards, right-of-way rules, speed limits. Mock theory test.

04
Defensive & distracted driving

Anticipating bike swerves, rickshaw lane changes, pedestrian behaviour. Phone-away driving habits.

05
Fire & safety information

In-car fire protocol. Seat-belt safety. Airbag basics. Child-seat guidance for parent learners.

06
Saturday workshop (120 min)

Tyre-change demo · Jump-start demo · First-aid · Completion certificate · Theory test · Q&A with guest speakers (Motorway / Highway officials when available).

B Behind-the-wheel progression (9 sessions)

01
Basics — quiet area near home

Clutch/brake/accelerator feel. Mirror-signal-maneuver drill. Steering control. Full-talk mode.

02
Phase streets & intersections

Low-traffic DHA/Clifton lanes. Hand-signal practice. Stop signs. Prompted mode — instructor cues, student acts.

03
Parking & reversing

Perpendicular parking. Parallel parking on narrow Jami/Ittehad Commercial streets. Reference-point technique.

04
Main roads & roundabouts

Shahrah-e-Firdousi, Korangi Road, Clifton Beach road. Lane discipline at speed. Roundabout etiquette.

05
Highway driving

Shahrah-e-Faisal, Northern Bypass segments. Merging, lane changes at 80+ km/h. Independent mode — student decides, instructor only intervenes on safety.

06
Night driving + rain

Visibility, headlight use, wet-road braking distance. Karachi monsoon conditions if in-season.
