Founder Story

Why Faryal started Seekho.

In 2019, after years in corporate marketing — Aman Foundation, Barclays, Hascol, GSK — Faryal Farooqui went looking for a driving school in Karachi that her female relatives would actually feel safe in. She couldn't find one.

Every option was the same story: a nervous learner paired with an impatient male instructor in a strange car in unfamiliar neighbourhoods. No theory. No structure. No sense of care. Just "steering wheel pakro aur chalao".

So she built the school she wanted to exist. A proper Driver's Education Programme — behind-the-wheel training combined with classroom sessions on vehicle anatomy, road safety, emergency handling. Female instructors for female learners who want them. Completion certificates. Dual-brake cars. Dashcams. Air-conditioning. All the things a 2019 driving school should have had and didn't.

Seekho was inaugurated in September 2019 by the Commissioner of Karachi, Iftikhar Ali Shallwani. Since then, 1,000+ students have learned to drive with us — most of them nervous, most of them now confident, and a lot of them women who had been told they'd never be "the driver" in their family.

— Faryal Farooqui, Founder & CEO

Our Mission

Three things we teach every student. In that order.

1

Confidence

Fear of driving is the real problem — not gear-shifts. We build confidence first. Skills follow naturally. Patient instructors, calm cars, your pace.

2

Safety

Dual-brake cars, dashcams, insured vehicles. Theoretical sessions on vehicle anatomy and emergencies — the things most schools skip. You leave knowing how to change a tyre and jump-start a car.

3

Courtesy

Karachi roads work when drivers respect each other. We teach road manners alongside road rules. Good drivers make better roads.

Our Approach

Theoretical + practical. Not one without the other.

Most driving schools in Karachi hand you keys and point at the road. We teach you what's under the hood, what the dashboard signs mean, and what to do when things go wrong — before you start the engine.

Theoretical curriculum

  • Introduction to Driver's Education (60-min online/on-site)
  • Vehicle anatomy — under the hood walkthrough
  • Dashboard signs + engine checks
  • Road safety signs + rules
  • Emergency protocols — tyre change, jump-start, accident
  • Defensive and distracted driving
  • Fire & safety information
  • Saturday group workshop (120 min) — theory test + certificate

Behind-the-wheel (BTW)

  • Start in a quiet area close to home
  • Full-talk → prompted → independent progression
  • City streets + Phase commercials
  • Main road + highway driving
  • Parallel parking in narrow DHA lanes
  • Night driving + rain conditions
  • 45-minute sessions, dual-brake vehicles
  • Dashcam-recorded for feedback
