What Seekho Offers

Why instructors stay with us.

Competitive Pay

Above-market per-lesson rates plus performance bonuses. We share tips and no-cancellation fees with the instructor, not the office.

Modern Fleet

Dual-brake Swift 2016/17 (manual) and Alto 2021 (automatic). Dashcams, insurance, first-aid kits, regular inspections — you're driving cars built for teaching.

Ongoing Training

Quarterly refreshers with Motorway Police trainers, defensive-driving modules, and first-aid certification. You become a better driver, not just a teacher.

Ladies-Friendly Environment

Majority-female team, female-only rest space at HQ, clear conduct SOPs. No one has to explain why this matters — we just got on with it in 2019.

Growing Brand

Press in Dawn, The Nation, Aurora, Biz Today. 1,000+ students and counting. Your work here travels — alumni hire you for corporate programmes.

Sunday Optional

Sessions run Mon–Sat and optional Sundays (with premium rates). Schedule flexibility around family time is real, not a slogan.
